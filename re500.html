HELVIX HVX-RE 500

Premium Pure Epoxy Anchoring Adhesive

Product Overview

Helvix HVX-RE 500 is a next-generation premium two-component pure epoxy anchoring adhesive engineered for the most demanding structural connections.

Designed for heavy-duty anchoring and post-installed rebar connections, Helvix establishes a new benchmark for reliability in extreme conditions.

Using advanced molecular resin architecture and proprietary low-temperature cross-linking technology, HVX-RE 500 delivers exceptional bond strength in cracked and uncracked concrete, masonry and solid rock.

Suitable for water-filled, submerged and low-temperature installations down to -5°C, making it ideal for critical structural applications.

Key Features

  • High Bond Strength
  • Cracked & Uncracked Concrete
  • Water-Filled Boreholes
  • Underwater Applications
  • Fast Load Transfer
  • Excellent Chemical Resistance
  • Suitable for Rebar Connections
  • Seismic C1 & C2 Applications
