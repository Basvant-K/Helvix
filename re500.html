HELVIX HVX-RE 500

Premium Two-Component Pure Epoxy Injection Mortar for Structural Anchoring and Post-Installed Rebar Connections.

Product Overview

Helvix HVX-RE 500 is a next-generation premium two-component pure epoxy injection mortar engineered for the most demanding structural anchoring and post-installed rebar connections.

Designed for heavy-duty anchoring and post-installed rebar connections, Helvix establishes a new benchmark for reliability in extreme conditions.

Using advanced molecular resin architecture and proprietary low-temperature cross-linking technology, HVX-RE 500 delivers exceptional bond strength in cracked and uncracked concrete, masonry and solid rock.

Suitable for water-filled, submerged and low-temperature installations down to -5°C, making it ideal for critical structural applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a pure epoxy injection mortar?

A pure epoxy injection mortar is a two-component structural adhesive designed for heavy-duty anchoring and post-installed reinforcing bar applications. It provides high bond strength and long-term durability.

Where can HELVIX HVX-RE 500 be used?

HELVIX HVX-RE 500 is suitable for cracked and uncracked concrete, natural stone, solid masonry, bridge construction, industrial plants, commercial buildings and infrastructure projects.

What is the difference between pure epoxy and vinylester?

Pure epoxy systems generally provide longer working time, excellent durability, high bond strength and are commonly selected for demanding structural anchoring applications.

Why choose HELVIX HVX-RE 500?

HELVIX HVX-RE 500 is designed for demanding structural anchoring applications requiring high bond strength, excellent durability, reliable long-term performance and suitability for cracked and uncracked concrete.
