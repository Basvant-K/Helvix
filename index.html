Our Products

HELVIX™ HVX - RE 500

Helvix™ HVX - RE 500 is a next-generation, premium two-component pure epoxy anchoring adhesive engineered for the most demanding structural connections. Designed for heavy-duty anchoring and post-installed rebar connections, Helvix sets a new industry standard for reliability in extreme conditions.

Utilizing an advanced molecular resin architecture and proprietary low-temperature cross-linking technology, Helvix achieves exceptional bond strength in cracked and uncracked concrete, masonry, and solid rock.

Engineered for Seismic C1/C2 conditions, dynamic loads, fire-rated structural doweling, water-saturated boreholes and underwater applications.

  • High Bond Strength
  • Suitable for Cracked & Uncracked Concrete
  • Water-Filled & Underwater Boreholes
  • Fast Load Transfer
  • Excellent Chemical Resistance
  • Suitable for Threaded Rods & Rebars
  • Engineered for Seismic C1 & C2
