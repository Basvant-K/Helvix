What is a Chemical Anchor?

A chemical anchor is a structural fastening system that bonds threaded rods or reinforcing bars into concrete using a high-strength adhesive instead of mechanical expansion.

What is Pure Epoxy Injection Mortar?

Pure epoxy injection mortar is a two-component structural adhesive used for heavy-duty anchoring, post-installed rebar connections and critical structural applications.

Applications

  • Bridge construction
  • Industrial plants
  • Commercial buildings
  • Infrastructure projects
  • Concrete strengthening
  • Rebar installation
  • Heavy machinery anchoring

Why Choose Chemical Anchors?

Chemical anchors provide high bond strength, excellent load distribution and reliable long-term performance. They are widely used for heavy-duty anchoring, post-installed reinforcing bars, steel structures, bridge construction and industrial equipment installation.
